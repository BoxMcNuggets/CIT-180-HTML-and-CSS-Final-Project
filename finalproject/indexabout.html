About

Squad Link is a application made in the C# language that allows people all over the world find other people with similar skill levels and knowledge of a game. The developer is the only one working on the app as a project outside of work to help others find people and enjoy games. This app allows the people to join a squad of people to play competitive games or competitive game modes built in games. The app is still in the process of being made but is the first of its kind to feature such a wide variety of games including big title games like Fortnite, Valorant, Tom Clancys: Rainbow 6 Siege, Counter Strike: Global Offence, Rocket League and more.
